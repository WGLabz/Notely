# AI Features

Once configured, AI utilities are available throughout the editor and search systems.

## 1. AI Chat Panel

Ask questions about your notes, outline new articles, or search for information:
1. Open the Command Palette (`Ctrl + K`).
2. Run **Open AI Chat Panel**.
3. Type your prompt. The chat assistant is aware of your workspace context and references relevant files.

---

## 2. AI Palette Actions

Refactor or rewrite text inside the editor:
1. Highlight the target text selection in the Markdown editor.
2. Press **`Ctrl + Space`** or right-click and select **AI Actions**.
3. Choose an action from the palette:
   - **Summarize**: Condenses selection into bullets.
   - **Improve Readability**: Smooths grammar and phrasing.
   - **Change Tone**: Switch to Professional, Casual, or Technical.
   - **Translate**: Translate text to other languages.

---

## 3. Semantic Search & Clustering

- **Intent Matching**: Search finds notes matching conceptual intent even if keywords differ.
- **Graph Grouping**: The Workspace Graph groups notes dynamically based on topics, highlighting related subject clusters.
- **Freshness Indicator**: The status bar shows whether semantic indexes are up-to-date or require a rebuild.
